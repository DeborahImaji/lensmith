A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Mandy Smith, a people & portrait photographer. I am very passionate about photography and it becomes quite obvious in the way I create portraits.

My work focuses on capturing honest expressions, subtle details, and the unique beauty in every person I photograph. I believe a great portrait tells a story, and I aim to create images that feel genuine, timeless, and full of life. Over the years, I’ve developed a style that balances natural light with creative direction, allowing every session to feel comfortable and effortless. Whether I’m working in the studio or on location, my goal is always the same — to create images that resonate, celebrate individuality, and preserve the moments that matter most.

I take photographs with creativity, concept & passion

MY SERVICES

I specialize in capturing the essence of people. Whether you require professional headshots, a comprehensive personal branding gallery, or timeless family and couples portraits, Lensmith offers a custom, comfortable experience. Let's create compelling images of your story.

People & Portrait Photography

I focus on genuine connection, crafting images that truly reflect you. This covers professional headshots, individual artistic portraits, and sessions for families and couples. I deliver clear, compelling, and memorable photography.

Advertorial Photography

Utilising portraits and environment, I create powerful visual content for brands, magazines, and marketing campaigns. These images tell a full story, highlighting your products or services through the lens of human connection and authentic, engaging lifestyle scenes.

Family Photography

Capturing your family's authentic connections, from the big milestones to the everyday chaos. My sessions are relaxed, fun, and focused on genuine moments, resulting in beautiful, candid photographs you can cherish as heirloom prints and digital memories forever.
